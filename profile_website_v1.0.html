Hobbie/Past Time #1 - Writing a novel📖✍️:

My first hobbie/past time is writing a novel📖✍️. The reason why I like writing novels is because I find that I can be very expressive when writing novels. I find that I can also change the story towards my liking just like how other authors or film directors like to change the story to their own personal tastes. Not only do I get to express myself by writing but I also get to write based on my own personal experience and what I've learnt in life through either the protagonist, antagonist or supporting characters instead of telling that own personal experience to readers through myself. I also like to write about not just my personal experience in life but also general experiences that we have all faced in life like maybe what guys have to go through in life or what girls have to go through in life and the reality that we all really face when living in this world. I specifically love writing about alternate history and multiverses as I find them really captivating the what ifs and the myseteries of the world mixed with a slice of life, romance, action and drama in my novel really helps me blend what I really love with the common genres of novels, movies and tv dramas (especially the Korean dramas as well!).

Hobbie/Past Time #2 - Watching shows and movies (like Netflix & YouTube)📺:

My second hobbie/past time is watching shows and movies📺. I like to watch shows on YouTube and Netflix and occasionally in the cinema as well with my friends. My fascination for foreign characters in a show tend to also put inspiration in my novels as well! Lastly, I feel that some movies speak to me and they actually talk about things that we can actually relate to based on what we have experienced in life and I think that is gets be pumped up as well.
